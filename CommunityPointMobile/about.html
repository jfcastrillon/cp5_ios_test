Mission

The mission of 2-1-1 Tampa Bay Cares, Inc. (2-1-1 TBC) is to create connections between people and resources. 2-1-1 TBC serves as a vital communication link between health and human services resources in Pinellas County and individuals seeking help. 2-1-1 TBC is the sole provider of information (gathering, sorting, validating, and distributing) for Pinellas County enabling those in need to quickly and efficiently access the health and human services delivery systems.
