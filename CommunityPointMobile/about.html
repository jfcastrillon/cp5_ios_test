Mission

The Heart of Illinois 2-1-1, a partnership between the Heart of Illinois United Way and Advanced Medical Transport of Central Illinois, offers 24/7 access for information and referral to community and social services throughout Peoria, Tazewell, Woodford, Marshall, Stark and Putnam counties.
